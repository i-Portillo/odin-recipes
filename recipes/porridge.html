Barley porridge

Basic recipe for a basic meal. Vikings were basic, what did you expect?

Ingredients

  • 3-4 dl coarse barley flour
  • 1 litre water
  • 2 apples and maybe a handful of hazelnuts
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Once the water boils, whisk down the flour a little at a time.
  3. Continue boiling for 5 minutes while continuously stirring.
  4. The apples and chopped nuts are boiled the last minutes.
  5. Take the pot off the fire and place it close to the fire for 30 minutes to an hour.

Notes

A bit of honey or butter on top of the porridge adds taste. The apples can be replaced or complemented with the fruits and berries of the season.

The water can partially or entirely be replaced with milk, but then the porridge burns more easily. If you have made butter the butter milk from that can be used.
